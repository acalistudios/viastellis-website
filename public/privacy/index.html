Privacy Policy

Effective July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information ACALI Studios (operator of ViaStellis) collects, how we use it, and your choices. We aim to collect only what we need to give you a meaningful, personal experience.

1. Information we collect

Account: your email address and display name.
Birth details: the date, time, and place of birth you provide, used to compute your astrological chart. You may mark your birth time as unknown.
Personalization (optional): if you turn on "Personalized" mode, you may share pronouns, focus areas, relationship/work/family status, and short notes through "Tell Stella about yourself" so Stella can tailor readings to you. This is entirely optional — ViaStellis works fine in "Chart only" mode, where none of this is collected. Anything you share here is never shared outside ViaStellis beyond the AI providers listed in Section 3 (used solely to generate your readings), and is never sold. You can view, edit, or permanently delete it yourself at any time in Settings — deletion is immediate, no need to contact us.
Your content: journal entries, saved charts, and questions you ask Stella.
Usage: basic logs of feature usage and credit activity needed to run the Service.
Payment: handled by Stripe — we receive confirmation and subscription status, but never your full card number.

2. How we use it

To create your chart and readings, operate AI features, personalize your experience if you opt in, process payments and credits, provide support, secure the Service, and comply with law. We do not sell your personal information, and we never share your personalization details or Stella's notes about you with anyone beyond the providers listed in Section 3.

3. Service providers

We share data only with providers that help us operate, under contract: Supabase (authentication and database hosting), Stripe (payments), and Google (Gemini API) and Anthropic (Claude API) (AI generation — your chart context, personalization details if provided, and questions are sent to generate Stella's responses). These providers process data on our behalf and do not use it for their own purposes.

4. Data retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. Personalization details and anything Stella remembers about you can be deleted by you, instantly, at any time — individually, or all at once by switching to "Chart only" mode in Settings. For everything else, you can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing Stella@viastellis.com. Deleting your account removes your charts, journal entries, personalization data, and profile, subject to limited records we must retain (e.g., payment records for accounting).

To request deletion of your account data: email Stella@viastellis.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm when complete.

5. Security

We use industry-standard measures including encryption in transit and row-level access controls so that your data is only accessible to you. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information.

6. Children

The Service is not directed to anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

7. Changes & contact

We may update this policy; we'll revise the "Effective" date above. Questions or requests: Stella@viastellis.com.
